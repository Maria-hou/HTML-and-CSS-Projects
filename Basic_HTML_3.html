Chinchillas are adorable rodents that many people keep as pets.

Chinchillas have an inquisitive and gentle nature, but also have a lot of energy. Therefore it would not be ideal for a family with young children to keep.

Chinchillas also require quite a bit of care. They need a well-balanced diet consisting of vegetables and fruits; and a suitable habitat that includes high quality bedding.

However, Chinchillas also need to take dust baths, which is one of the cutest things you will ever witness.
